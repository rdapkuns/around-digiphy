CONCEPT STATION

Adding accessories

When I'm designing a new car, the physical experience matters just as much as the digital one. That's why I create extra physical add-ons - 3D printed or modelled - that I can mount directly onto the DigiPHY frame to see how they fit and feel in the interior.
