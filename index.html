Adjustable seats

In Digiphy, the seats are fully adjustable. I can change their dimensions ansd angles. Overall feel to match whatever interior I'm designing. Whether the project needs something sporty, compact, or more relaxed, I just shape the seat to fit the car's character, make them and drop it straight into DigiPHY.

Steering wheel

The steering wheel in Digiphy is fully adjustable, so I can match it to any type of car I'm developing. I can shift the height, widen the stance, or tighten it up depending on whether I'm working on

Alignment cameras

The alignment cameras help me bring the digital layer into the real setup. They make sure every virtual element sits exactly where it should, so the whole experience lines up perfectly.

Pedals

The pedals in Digiphy are fully adjustable, so I can set them up to match the driving position of any type of car. I can move them closer, lower, wider,... Whatever the layout needs.

The first steps

When we kick off a new car design, we always start with sketching. It's the quickest way to explore vibes, shapes, and attitude. Once the vision feels right on paper, we jump into 3D to bring those lines to life.

Here you can select which components you want to use and customize them to match your preferred setup.

Adding accessories

When I'm designing a new car, the physical experience matters just as much as the digital one. That's why I create extra physical add-ons - 3D printed or modelled - that I can mount directly onto the DigiPHY frame to see how they fit and feel in the interior.

Press the Headset button to put the VR goggles on. The car will come to life in front of you, perfectly aligned with the physical setup

Digital layer

DigiPHY phyisical aspect is important, but the digital layer is where everything comes together. Using DigiPHY's special software on any device, I can see my car's setup and control the phisical components with a single tap. The system allows me to change and adjust the phyiscal object dimensions in a matter of seconds.
